# Phase 24: Polish & White-Label Branding

**Milestone:** v1.5 Production-Ready Client Portal
**Goal:** Professional branded experience that reflects Qualia Solutions brand identity
**Priority:** P3 - Nice to have, enhances perceived value
**Plans:** 2 plans in 1 wave

## Context

Every competitor research source highlights white-labeling as a key differentiator. Current portal shows:

- "Qualia Internal Suite" branding (feels like internal tool)
- Generic welcome messages
- No custom domain or branded experience

For premium client perception, portal should feel like a dedicated Qualia Solutions client experience.

## Success Criteria

**What must be TRUE after completion:**

1. **Branded login experience** - Custom login page with Qualia Solutions branding
2. **Professional portal identity** - Portal shows "Qualia Solutions Client Portal" not internal tool
3. **Custom welcome messages** - Personalized greetings and project-specific messaging
4. **Consistent brand experience** - Matches Qualia Solutions website/proposal aesthetic

## Plans

### Plan 24-01: Portal Branding & Identity

**Goal:** Transform portal from internal tool to professional client experience
**Tasks:**

- Rebrand portal header, sidebar, login page with Qualia Solutions identity
- Create custom domain setup (portal.qualiasolutions.net)
- Design branded email templates for all notifications
- Add custom favicon, meta tags, and professional messaging throughout

### Plan 24-02: Personalization & Premium Touches

**Goal:** Add personalized elements that show attention to client relationship
**Tasks:**

- Custom welcome messages per client/project
- Client logo upload and display in portal header
- Personalized project naming and custom project descriptions
- "Behind the scenes" work visibility in activity feed

## Dependencies

- All previous phases (foundation functionality must work)
- Qualia Solutions brand guidelines and assets

## Risk Assessment

**Low risk** - Mostly design and content work, no complex functionality

## Validation

**Brand experience test:**

1. Client receives portal credentials → login page feels professional and branded
2. Portal experience matches quality expectation from Qualia website/proposals
3. Client sees personalized elements (their project name, custom messaging)
4. Portal feels like premium service, not generic tool
