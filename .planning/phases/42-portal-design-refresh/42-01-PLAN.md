Redesign the sidebar with these specific changes:

**Background**: Change `bg-card/50` to `bg-[#EDF0F0] dark:bg-[#121819]` — the teal-tinted surface color from Impeccable v4.0. Not `bg-card` (too generic), not transparent — the actual tinted background.

**NavLink active state**: Keep the left accent bar (it's good). Change active background from `bg-primary/[0.08]` to `bg-primary/[0.06] dark:bg-primary/[0.10]` (slightly softer). Inactive hover: change from `hover:bg-foreground/[0.04]` to `hover:bg-primary/[0.04]` — tint the hover toward brand.

**Bottom user section**: Add `backdrop-blur-sm` to the border-t container. Change `border-border` to `border-primary/10 dark:border-primary/15` for a tinted separator. Add a very subtle `bg-primary/[0.02]` background tint to the container div.

**User avatar initial circle**: Upgrade from `bg-primary/10` to `bg-gradient-to-br from-primary/15 to-primary/8` with `ring-1 ring-primary/20`. Size up from `h-7 w-7` to `h-8 w-8`, font from `text-[11px]` to `text-xs`.
