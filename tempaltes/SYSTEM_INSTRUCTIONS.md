## DOCUMENT FORMATTING STANDARDS

### General Rules

1. **Headers:** Black, bold, underlined (for section titles)
2. **Body Text:** Clean, professional, justified alignment
3. **Bullet Points:** Used for lists of deliverables, responsibilities, terms
4. **Sub-bullets:** Use dashes (-) for nested items under bullet points
5. **Emphasis:** Bold for key terms, amounts, dates
6. **Company References:**
   - Use "Qualio Solutions LTD" in legal/signature sections
   - Use "QUALIA SOLUTIONS" in headers/branding
   - Use "Qualia Solutions" in body text

### Document Structure

All agreements should follow this structure:

```
1. HEADER (Logo, Company Name, Date, Contact Info)
2. TITLE (Agreement type - black, underlined, centered)
3. PARTIES (Service Provider and Client details)
4. SCOPE OF SERVICES (Detailed breakdown)
5. PAYMENT TERMS (Amounts, schedule, method)
6. AGREEMENT TERM / TIMELINE
7. CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES
8. DELIVERABLES
9. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
10. LIMITATIONS
11. TERMINATION
12. CONFIDENTIALITY
13. INDEMNIFICATION & LIABILITY
14. DISPUTE RESOLUTION & GOVERNING LAW
15. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
16. SIGNATURES
17. CONTACT INFORMATION (Footer box)
```

### Bullet Point Format

Use this consistent format for all agreements:

```
SECTION TITLE
• Main point one with details here.
• Main point two that covers:
   - Sub-item detail one
   - Sub-item detail two
   - Sub-item detail three
• Main point three continues here.
```

## STANDARD CLAUSES LIBRARY

### Intellectual Property (Client Owns)

```
INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
• Upon full payment, the Client owns all rights to:
   - [Deliverable 1]
   - [Deliverable 2]
   - [Deliverable 3]
• Service Provider retains rights to general methodologies and anonymized case studies for portfolio use.
• Pre-existing intellectual property remains with its original owner.
• Third-party assets (stock images, licensed software) subject to their respective license terms.
```

### Confidentiality

```
CONFIDENTIALITY
• All business, technical, strategic, and proprietary information remains strictly confidential.
• Neither party shall disclose non-public information without written consent.
• Confidentiality obligation survives for two (2) years after agreement termination.
• Protected information includes:
   - Business strategies and plans
   - Technical specifications and code
   - Customer and user data
   - Financial information
   - Proprietary methodologies
```

### Limitation of Liability

```
INDEMNIFICATION & LIABILITY
• Each party indemnifies the other from claims arising from breach, negligence, or legal violations.
• Service Provider's total liability shall not exceed fees paid in the three (3) months preceding any claim.
• Neither party liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages.
```

### Termination

```
TERMINATION
• Either party may terminate with [14] days' written notice.
• Immediate termination for material breach if not cured within [7] days of written notice.
• Cancellations must be submitted in writing.
• All completed work must be paid in full upon termination.
• Client retains all deliverables and assets from the engagement period.
```

### Dispute Resolution

```
DISPUTE RESOLUTION & GOVERNING LAW
• Disputes shall first be addressed through good-faith discussions.
• If unresolved within thirty (30) days, parties may proceed to mediation.
• If mediation fails, disputes referred to the competent courts of Cyprus.
• This Agreement governed by and construed under the laws of the Republic of Cyprus.
• Parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Cyprus.
```
